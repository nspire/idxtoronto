About Nspire IDX

Ideas Exchange is about encouraging deep thinking to observe and analyze the challenging conditions in our digital age. It's your chance to interact with professionals who have expert knowledge in areas relevant to you and to expand your proverbial toolbelt as a young entrepreneur.

Since 2001, Nspire has organized numerous events across university campuses in Ontario. Ideas Exchange is one of the most respected forums of its kind: specialized in connecting university students with people who have faced the challenges in the business and technology field.

Our Vision

To inspire in them the confidence to take on challenges and opportunities

To empower them with skills and knowledge enabling them to be distinguished as innovative leaders

To connect youth leaders with industry professionals and entrepreneurs

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Nspire?

    Nspire Innovation Network is Canada’s largest student-run non-profit organization in the business and technology space. Our mission is to connect students and professionals with industry leaders, empower their potential as innovative leaders and inspire action at events and opportunities all throughout the year.

  • What is Ideas Exchange Toronto?

    Ideas Exchange Toronto is Nspire’s Toronto based initiative. Our aim is to connect entrepreneurial students with experienced industry leaders in order to facilitate idea-generation and discussion about challenges faced by modern-day businesses. Delegates get the chance to learn, network and create lasting connections with other biz tech ballers.

  • Why should I come to your Tech Talk?

    • Quality opportunities to network with industry experts and top company recruiters.

    • Connect with people who share the same interests and get put yourself out there.

    • Your dinner is on us!

  • Why do you need my resume?

    One of the best parts about attending Nspire events is the opportunity to network with our partners. In order to best facilitate this, our partners ask us to relay attendee resumes. Only attendees who submit a resume are potential candidates for career opportunities with our partners.
